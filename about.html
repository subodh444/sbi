ABOUT US

Philosophy


"We believe that a prosperous and harmonious society is made possible by equitable distribution of resources and opportunities. High and persistent levels of inequality means that the talents and potential of most people remain underdeveloped and unfulfilled, resulting in a loss to individuals, communities and society. Sanghamitra works on achieving this vision of an equal society by partnering with individuals and organizations to address and remedy gaps in experience, skills, technology, and capital.



Practice


As an incubator, our focus is on growing socially conscious businesses and organizations. We work on all stages of this process identifying, partnering, launching, operating and scaling. Typically, we partner with creative talents (makers, artists, authors, entrepreneurs) and organizations to develop their market reach with products and services. We have an emphasis on developing local talents and global networks with close mentoring and apprenticeships. Starting with a few business lines, were actively working on expanding to more areas. Our consumer line of products are available on our store website. A learning platform developed by us as a strategy and technology partner can be explored at www.digitalnalanda.com


Method


These are some key words in our lexicon: Risk taking. Skill building. Technology adoption. Continuous iteration. Rapid delivery. Value creation. These are some key questions we ask and work on daily: What will it take for a maker or artist to be a business owner? How does a novice entrepreneur launch new products and business lines? What will it take to scale reach and production? How can associates gain skills valued in the market/economy? What skills do they need to explore and develop? How can they help train apprentices – the next maker, artist, entrepreneur? Which technologies can expand their scope and effectiveness?

Habitat


The magic of creation. The logistics of production. The mystery of the market. We embrace it all in our adventures together. Along with our associates, we co-create an environment for informed risk taking and rapid skill building based on real world engagements, multidimensional exploration and trial and error experimentation. We strive to d o this in a way that is personally meaningful and enjoyable for each individual and promotes their well-being and growth. If our vision and method resonates with you, we invite you to reach out and explore possibilities.






Sanghamitra is a business incubator which aims to engage the creative energies of local communities in building socially conscious business practices and products
